About Me

I am Kaviarasu K, a passionate video editor specializing in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects. With a blend of creativity and technical expertise, I transform raw footage into compelling, cinematic stories that engage and captivate audiences.

My Journey


My editing journey began during my diploma, where I discovered how much impact visual storytelling can have. Over time, I worked on short films, product teasers, and creative YouTube content. Alongside editing, I also gained experience in sound design and motion graphics. Currently, I am studying B.Tech CSE, which allows me to merge my love for technology with creative video editing.

Vision


My vision is to create cutting-edge video content that blends cinematic storytelling with innovative editing techniques. I focus on leveraging AI, automation, and real-time tools to deliver immersive and engaging experiences for audiences across platforms.
